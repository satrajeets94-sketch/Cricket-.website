My Hobby – Cricket

About Cricket

Cricket is my favourite sport. I love playing both batting and bowling. It teaches teamwork, discipline, and focus. I enjoy watching cricket matches and learning new techniques from professional players.

Why I Love Cricket

I love cricket because it keeps me active, improves my fitness, and helps me stay confident. Playing cricket with friends makes me happy and reduces stress. I also follow international matches and support my favourite teams.
